    <h2 id="timeline"><strong>Biography</strong></h2> 
    <ul>
<li><strong>1809:</strong> Born in Shrewsbury, Shropshire, England, on 12 February 1809 at his family's home, The Mount.</li>
<li><strong>1817:</strong> Death of his mother.</li>
<li><strong>1825:</strong> Beginning of his medical studies at the University of Edinburgh Medical School</li>
<li><strong>1828:</strong> Beginning of his theology studies at Christ's College, Cambridge to become a parson afterwards.</li>
<li><strong>1831:</strong> Receipt of his theology degree.</li>
<li><strong>1831:</strong> 1831: Voyage of the HMS Beagle begins (December, 27).</li>
<li><strong>1836:</strong> Return of the HMS Beagle at Falmouth, Cornwall.</li>
<li><strong>1839:</strong> Marriage of Emma Wedgewood.</li>
<li><strong>1859:</strong>  Publication of <em>On the Origin of Species</em></li>
<li><strong>1871:</strong> Publication of <em>The Descent of Man, and Selection in Relation to Sex</em></li>
<li><strong>1882:</strong> Death by heart failure on 19 April 1882.</li>

    </ul>
